**Medium** | **25 minutes** | **String, Dynamic Programming**

## Examples

### Example 1

**Input:** `s = "babad"`

**Output:** `"bab"`

**Input:** `s = "cbbd"`

**Output:** `"bb"`

## Constraints

- `1 <= s.length <= 1000`
